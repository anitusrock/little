Admission Overview

Admissions at Silverlines Matriculation School are open to students who seek quality education in a disciplined and nurturing environment. The school follows a transparent and systematic admission process aimed at identifying students who are willing to learn and grow academically and personally. The institution welcomes students from diverse backgrounds and ensures equal learning opportunities for all. Admissions are granted based on availability of seats and fulfillment of eligibility criteria prescribed by the school and Matriculation Board norms.

Classes Offered

Admissions are offered for the following classes subject to seat availability:

  • Pre-Kindergarten (Pre-KG)
  • Lower Kindergarten (LKG)
  • Upper Kindergarten (UKG)
  • Primary Classes (I to V)
  • Middle School (VI to VIII)
  • Secondary Level (IX & X)

Each level is designed to provide age-appropriate learning experiences and academic progression.

Eligibility Criteria

Students seeking admission must satisfy the age and academic requirements prescribed for the respective class. Admission to higher classes is based on the successful completion of the previous standard from a recognized school. Transfer Certificate
