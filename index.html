Vision, Mission & Core Values

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in education that nurtures knowledge, character, and leadership, empowering students to become responsible global citizens and future leaders.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating learning environment that promotes academic excellence, moral values, discipline, and life skills through innovative teaching methods and holistic development programs, enabling every student to realize their fullest potential.

Discipline

Self-control, punctuality, and responsible behaviour.

Integrity

Honesty, transparency, and strong moral principles.

Respect

Mutual respect for teachers, peers, and society.

Chairman's Message

Mr. K. Sankar, B.B.A., B.L.

"Education is the most powerful foundation for building a strong and progressive society. At Silverlines, our vision is to provide meaningful and value-based education to every child. We believe true education shapes character, discipline, and responsibility."

Our institution is committed to creating a learning environment where students are encouraged to think independently, act responsibly, and grow confidently. With dedicated faculty and modern teaching practices, we nurture young minds to face future challenges with courage and integrity.

Secretary's Message

Mrs. Pugalvizhi Sankar, M.Sc., M.Ed.

"We believe that education should nurture both the intellect and the character of every child. Our goal is to create a positive and student-friendly learning environment where children feel motivated to achieve their fullest potential."

We focus on academic excellence while emphasizing discipline, values, and personality development. Each child is unique, and our approach encourages individual growth through guidance, care, and continuous encouragement. Together, we build a strong foundation for success.

Guidelines

Rules & Regulations

Discipline & Conduct

Students are expected to maintain respect, responsibility, and positive behaviour both within and outside the campus.

Attendance

Regular and punctual attendance is essential. Leave requests must be submitted in writing by parents.

Uniform Policy

Prescribed school uniforms must be worn neatly, representing discipline and equality among all students.

Education is a joint responsibility. We value parent cooperation in monitoring progress and maintaining discipline.
